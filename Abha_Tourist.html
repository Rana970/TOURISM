Abha Tourist City

Abha Tourist City: The Saudi city of Abha, which is located in the southwest of the Kingdom, is famous for being one of the tourist destinations that visitors come to in the summer. It has achieved tourism fame in a short time, as the number of tourists there in 2017 reached about 4.4 million tourists, and in that year it obtained .The title of the Arab Tourism Capital Award by the Arab Tourism Organization of the League of Arab States. Among the tourism components of the city of Abha are its cold weather, moderate rains, and picturesque mountainous landscapes, which provide the opportunity to establish four cable car systems to connect the city’s resorts, including the connection between the rocky slope in Souda. Al-Habla, the lake area, and some of the mountains of the new city of Abha. In addition, the city of Abha has a rich cultural and traditional heritage, and architectural diversity that includes mosaic works, thanks to the city’s long history and being one of the most densely populated areas in the Arabian Peninsula region. . The development of tourism in the city of Abha characterized by amazing geological diversity, from amazing rock formations between desert valleys, and natural wonders towering above the sand dunes.
